\reviewsection{Summary of the 10-Minute Lesson Taught}

The lesson was titled \textit{Many Ways to Be a Good Classmate}. The student-friendly goal was: \textit{I can name two ways to include and support a classmate who communicates, learns, moves, or experiences the room differently.}

I began with a visible first/next/last sequence. First, students learned one respectful idea about autism. Next, they practiced with a familiar classroom situation. Last, they chose one supportive action they could use. This predictable structure reflected my placement observations
