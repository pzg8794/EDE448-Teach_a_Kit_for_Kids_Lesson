I designed the lesson for a Grade 2 technology setting because my placement repeatedly showed that peer support becomes visible during coding, robotics, and collaborative building. Students needed help with login sequences, links, unfamiliar programs, materials, noise, turn-taking, and uneven pacing.

In one ocasion, a student who knew how to enter a program was encouraged to help classmates who were still struggling. Later, a student questioned whether a visitor counted as a classmate, and the response emphasized that the person should still be included and should not be made to feel bad \citep{placement2026robotics}. These moments made belonging a real classroom issue rather than an abstract idea.
